Hotdog Recipe

Ingredients

  • Hotdog Meat (any brand)
  • Hotdog Bun
  • Ketchup/Mustard
  • Any other condiments you like!

Recipe Instructions

  1. Grill the hotdog on one side until grill marks show up (about 2-3 minutes).
  2. Flip the hotdog and repeat.
  3. Optional: Toast the bun on the grill.
  4. Place the dog in the bun and top with any condiments you like!
