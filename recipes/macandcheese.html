Mac and Cheese Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Cheese

Recipe Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta, drain.
  2. Make the cheese sauce by combing butter and flour, then whisking in milk products.
  3. Cook the sauce until it's nice and thick.
  4. Add in shredded cheeses, stir well.
  5. Combine the cheese sauce and cooked pasta.
  6. Transfer half of the mac and cheese to a baking dish, sprinkle with more shredded cheese.
  7. Bake and Enjoy!
